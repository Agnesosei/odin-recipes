Ghana Jollof


Description

Jollof rice is a special meal among Ghanaians.
There is never a wedding, never a funeral nor even a simple family gathering
without our precious jollof.

If you are ever in Ghana, you should try our hollof. Of course it wont be difficult to find...
every food joint has it for breakfast, lunch or supper; we eat jollof all the time!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Tomato puree (fresh or canned)
  • Habenero pepper to taste
  • Salt
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Vegetable/coconut oil
  • Chicken, Meat or Fish

Steps

  • Wash all vegetables and meat
  • Blend onions, garlic and preferred spices and pour over meat
    (skip this step if uising fish). Add salt and steam meat/chicken over low heat until tender.
  • Fry meat/ chicken/ fish and set aside
  • Blend some tomates, pepper, garlic and onions and set aside
  • Heat some oil and add sliced onions. Cook until caramelised.
    Add tomato puree and stir for 5 mins. Add meat/chicken/fish and let it cook for 5 minutes.
    You can choose to take out your fish and set aside at this point (unitl serving time).
    Add your rice to the stew. Pour enough water to cover the rice and add salt to taste.
    Stir once and leave to cook on low heat under tight lid for 15-20 minutes.
