Waakye


Description

Waakye, like jollof, is a favourite in Ghana.

If you are ever in Ghana, you should try our waakye. Of course it wont be difficult to find...
every food joint has it for breakfast, lunch or supper; we eat waakye all the time!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Beans (black-eyed peas)
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  • Wash and soak beans for about 30 minutes.
  • Drain water and boil with fresh water until slightly tender.
  • Add washed rice and cover
  • Leave to cook on low heat for about 2o minutes until tender
  • Serve with tomato stew and fish with some salad
